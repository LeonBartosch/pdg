In this study, you have the chance to receive a bonus payment based on the decisions in the study, in addition to your standard Prolific payment. You will receive a bonus payment based on the decisions you make.

How is the bonus calculated?

  • You will receive a bonus for a randomly selected task.
  • The bonus depends on the task and on your answers and/or the other randomly selected participant.
  • The amount of the bonus payment can vary between 0 and 15 Talers.
  • 1 Taler is equivalent to 10 cents and 15 Talers are equivalent to 150 Cents.

Please note, neither you nor the other randomly selected participant will be made aware of each other's identity.
