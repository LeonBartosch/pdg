Overview of the study

In this study, you will be asked to take part in a couple of decision-making tasks.

For each task, we will explain the general structure of this task and provide some specific instructions. Before moving forward with the task, we will check whether our instructions are clear through some questions. Last, we will ask you for some information about yourself.

You have the chance to receive a bonus payment based on the decisions in the study. We will provide details in the instructions.
