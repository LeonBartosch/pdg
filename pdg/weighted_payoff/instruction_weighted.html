You can earn a bonus payment based on the decisions you and the other participants make. Participants in this study are either socially close or socially distant to you. The social closeness between yourself and your interaction partner impacts your bonus payment:

  • Interactions with socially close participants contribute more to your bonus:
    • Each close participant contributes 40% (total 80%).
  • Interactions with socially distant contribute less:
    • Each distant participant contributes 10% (total 20%).
  • Group membership ({{ player.participant.color }}) does not affect how much participants contribute to your bonus.
Hence, your total bonus will be calculated based on all of these interactions, with socially closer participants having a greater impact on your final bonus.

The rules for the other players are the same, so that the Talers you send also have a greater impact on the bonus of close others as compared to distant others.
