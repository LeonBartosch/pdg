You can earn a bonus payment based on the decisions you and the other participants make. Only one interaction will be randomly selected to determine your bonus payment. The social closeness of each participant influences the chance of a task being chosen:

  • Interactions with socially close participants have a chance of 80% to be selected for payment.
    • Each close participant has a 40% chance of being selected (total 80%).
  • Interactions with socially distant participants have a chance of 20% to be selected for payment.
    • Each distant participant has a 10% chance of being selected (total 20%).
  • Group membership (blue / orange) does not affect the chance of a task being chosen.
So overall, your bonus will be determined by one of the choices you made. Choices with close others have a higher likelihood of being selected compared to choices with distant others.

The rules for the other players are the same, so that the Talers you send also have a higher likelihood to be selected as bonus of close others as compared to distant others.
